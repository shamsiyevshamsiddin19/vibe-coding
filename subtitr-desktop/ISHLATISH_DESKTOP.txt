Subtitr Desktop — o'rnatish va ishlatish
========================================

O'RNATISH
---------
1. SubtitrSetup.exe faylini yuklab oling (Telegram yoki internetdan).
2. Ustiga ikki marta bosing va "O'rnatish" ni bosing.
   - Administrator huquqi shart emas, faqat shu foydalanuvchi uchun o'rnatiladi.
3. O'rnatilgach dastur o'zi ochiladi (yoki ish stolidagi/menyudagi
   "Subtitr Desktop" yorlig'idan oching).

API KALIT KIRITISH (bir marta)
------------------------------
Dastur AI xizmatlaridan foydalanadi. Har kim O'ZINING kalitini kiritadi:
1. Yuqori o'ngdagi kalit (🔑) ikonkasini bosing.
2. Kamida bittasini kiriting:
   - Groq  — BEPUL, tez. https://console.groq.com/keys
   - OpenAI — eng sifatli tarjima. https://platform.openai.com/api-keys
   - Gemini — muqobil. https://aistudio.google.com/apikey
3. "Saqlash" ni bosing. Kalit faqat shu kompyuterda saqlanadi.

Kalitsiz ham ishlaydi, lekin faqat video yonidagi tayyor .srt yoki
cheklangan oflayn lug'at bilan.

ISHLATISH
---------
1. Videoni "Videolar" (Videos) papkasiga tashlang yoki "Tashqi video"
   tugmasi orqali istalgan joydan tanlang.
2. Rejimni tanlang (tarjima+lug'at, ikki qatorli subtitr, SRT, matn, h.k.).
3. Video tili va tarjima tilini tanlang.
4. "Boshlash" ni bosing.
5. Tayyor bo'lgach "Natijalar" tugmasi natija papkasini ochadi:
   Videolar\Subtitr natijalar\<video nomi>

TRANSKRIPSIYA TARTIBI
---------------------
1) Video yonidagi .srt/.vtt   2) Videoning ichki subtitri
3) Groq Whisper (kalit bo'lsa)

TARJIMA TARTIBI
---------------
OpenAI -> Gemini -> Groq (qaysi kalit bo'lsa) -> oflayn lug'at (zaxira).

ESLATMA
-------
- ffmpeg/ffprobe dastur ichida keladi, alohida o'rnatish shart emas.
- Ish fayllari: %LOCALAPPDATA%\SubtitrDesktop
- O'chirish: Parametrlar > Ilovalar > Subtitr Desktop, yoki menyudagi
  "Subtitr Desktop ni o'chirish".
