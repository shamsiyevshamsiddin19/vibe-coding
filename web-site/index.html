ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Shamsiddin — a backend developer from Tashkent. I got into programming in August 2024 and have been hooked ever since, learning the craft under the mentorship of Nurulloh Muhammadov. Most of what I build lives on the server side: Telegram bots, APIs, and the databases and Linux servers behind them.

I like shipping things people actually use — like Subtitr Bot, my AI subtitle bot — and I lean on my background in higher mathematics to reason through algorithms. I read English and Russian documentation comfortably, so I rarely stay stuck for long.
